SHEE.APP is a presentation app that enables interactive communication between presenters and participants. The app has two different views: one for the presenter and one for the participants. Currently, the presenter can choose from three different modules:
  • Polling: The presenter initiates a poll that participants can view and vote on.
  • Pairing: Provides participants with a QR code to easily join the correct presenter room. It also keeps track of the number of participants currently listening to the presenter.
  • Brainstorming: Implements an infinite Whiteboard to collect ideas from participants. Participants can also vote on these ideas.
To get started, you need to enter a room name. After that, you will be redirected to a page where you can choose the type of interaction you want to have with your participants. Participants can join the room using the link provided above.
Please note that the current design is in its initial developmental phase. Every component and visual element you see is in a preliminary stage and serves as a starting point for future iterations.
